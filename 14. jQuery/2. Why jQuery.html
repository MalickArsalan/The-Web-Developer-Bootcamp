  3. Why would you use jQuery
  4. Why would you not use jQuery

Why Use jQuery

  • Fixes "broken" DOM API
  • Brevity and Clarity
  • Ease of use
  • Cross-Browser Support
  • AJAX
  • Lot's of people use jQuery!

Why Not Use jQuery

  • The DOM API is no longer "broken"
  • It doesn't do anything ou can't do on your own
  • It's an unnecessary dependency
  • Performance
  • Lots's of people are moving away from jQuery!
